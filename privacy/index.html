Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

This application does not collect, store, or sell any personal data from its users.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how this application ("the App") handles information when you authorize it to connect to your Instagram account via the Instagram Basic Display API and Instagram Graph API.

The App is used solely for the purpose of publishing content to Instagram through Postiz, a social media scheduling tool.

2. Data We Access

In order to post content on your behalf, the App requests the following permissions from Instagram:

  • Access to your Instagram account (username, profile ID)
  • Permission to publish media and posts to your account

This access is granted only with your explicit consent via Instagram's OAuth authorization flow.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Personal identification information
  • Passwords or credentials
  • Messages or private content
  • Follower lists or contact data
  • Financial information

4. Analytics

The App may use basic, anonymized analytics (e.g., page views) to understand usage patterns. This data is not linked to any individual user and is not shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

This App uses the Instagram Graph API provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. Your use of Instagram is governed by Meta's own Privacy Policy.

Content scheduling is facilitated via Postiz. Please refer to Postiz's privacy policy for information on how they handle your data.

6. Data Retention

We do not store your Instagram access tokens or any account data beyond the active session required to publish content.

7. Your Rights

You may revoke access to your Instagram account at any time by visiting:

Instagram → Settings → Apps and Websites

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
